Privacy Policy for eRural

Last updated: September 7, 2025

eRural is built to connect rural communities by providing village information, jobs, agriculture updates, and community posts.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number for authentication
  • Profile details (name, photo, village selection)
  • User-generated content (posts, photos, comments)
  • Device identifiers (Firebase Cloud Messaging token for notifications)

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Deliver village-specific news, jobs, and agriculture updates
  • Send important notifications and alerts
  • Maintain app security and prevent fraud

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may use trusted third-party services such as Google Firebase to provide hosting, authentication, and messaging.

Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your personal information through the app settings or by contacting us directly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at: support@eruralapp.com
